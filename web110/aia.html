AIA: Audience, Issues, Approach

Client Information
Client Name & final decision maker Michelle C.
Business Name Stella Rose Designs - Upcycled and refurbished custom home designs.
Re-design or New New
Type of Website Identity, Marketing, Shopping
Client's Goals To market and advertise new products for sale via appointment, display examples of previous work, create an online presence, grow sales, and provide multiple ways to contact Michelle as this will not be an e-commerce site or storefront.
Audience Information
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Men & Women 20-50 Years Old
Typically viewing on mobile devices and tablets. They are moderately to highly tech savvy, active on social media, and seeking upcycled products.
  • Contact info/how to contact
  • Ability to view on mobile devices
  • What products and services are offered
  • How to buy items
  • Create a responsive website that's compatible with all device types & screen resolutions.
  • Modern Website Design.
  • Social Media Links.
  • Multiple ways to contact Michelle (links to contact form throughout pages as well as social media links).
Men & Women Ages 50+
Typically viewing on a desktop or laptop computer at home (Mac and Windows). Basic to moderate computer skills and not highly active on social media.
  • Contact info/how to contact
  • Site navigation/ability to find information
  • What products and services are offered
  • How to buy items
  • An easy to use contact form.
  • Contact information will be a clear, main focus of website design and put in multiple places.
  • Easy to navigate website made possible by clearly identifying site links and making items and descriptions easily accessible.
  • How to buy items will be explained in an "About Me" paragraph.
People Seeking Custom Pieces
All ages and platforms potentially seeking a custom furniture or accent piece.
  • Contact info/how to contact
  • What custom services are provided
  • Pricing information
  • Multiple contact links within site.
  • Custom services will be explained in a short paragraph located at the bottom of the page or in a "FAQ" section.
  • Pricing will vary based on customer request and will be discussed via email or phone - no pricing given on website. "Contact For Pricing Inquiries" link will be provided.
